Multimodal Freight Services

Over the road, through the air, or on the rails, Summit Logistics provides a complete array of multimodal transportation services. With a reputation for on-time, door-to-door delivery and intense customer service.

We help companies in a wide range of industries get their products to market with speed, efficiency, and accuracy.

We're proud of our consistent execution of shipments. Our resourceful, creative, knowledgeable team will meet your needs by leveraging a strong blend of our assets, and non-asset based resources through carrier partnerships. With high safety ratings and an on-time percentage in 2010 at nearly 99%, we'll work hard to make sure your shipments arrive on time, every time.

Our Services at a Glance:

  • Truckload and LTL, involving refrigerated, temperature-controlled, and dry freight.
  • Expedited, using either over-the-road or air transportation capabilities.
  • Intermodal, through strategic partnerships with Class I railroads
  • Air Cargo, both domestic and international.
  • Warehousing, with capabilities to store temperature-controlled or dry products.
